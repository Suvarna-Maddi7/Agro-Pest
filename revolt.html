INSECTICIDE

Revolt

General Info

Technical Name:Fipronil 5% S.C

Major Crops:Fruits,Vegetables,Cotton,Rice,Tea.

Mode of action:It works by binding to chloride channels in the nerve and muscle cells of the pest, which increases the flow of

chloride ions and disrupts normal nerve impulses. This results in paralysis and eventually death of the insect.

Target Pest:Bollworms,Fruit borers,leaf miners,Diamondback moth,Tomato leaf miner,Lepidopteran pests.

Presentation Available: 100ml,250ml,500ml, 1ltr.

Company:Preventis
