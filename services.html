Frequently asked questions

Short answers to common farmer queries.

How do drone charges work?

We charge per acre. The exact quote depends on area, application type, and travel. We provide transparent estimates before work.

Are your products safe for beneficial insects?

We recommend integrated pest management — selective products and timing to preserve beneficials. Our agronomists advise the best plan per crop.

How quickly can I get support?

Our helpline is 24/7 and local field teams typically respond within 24 hours for service requests in our core service area.
