Effective Pest Traps

Advanced solutions for monitoring and controlling pest populations. Sticky traps capture insects with a sticky surface, often brightly colored to attract pests like whiteflies and aphids. Pheromone traps lure specific species using synthetic pheromones, ideal for agricultural use. Light traps attract night-flying insects with UV light, commonly used in agricultural and residential settings. Pitfall traps capture ground-dwelling insects by trapping them in containers buried at ground level. Live traps safely capture larger pests like rodents, allowing for their relocation.
